POLICE SIREN SOUND FILES
========================

Add the following sound files to this folder:

Required files (OGG or MP3 format):
- siren1.ogg  - Wail siren (long rising/falling tone)
- siren2.ogg  - Yelp siren (fast alternating tone)
- siren3.ogg  - Hi-Lo siren (European style two-tone)
- horn.ogg    - Air horn blast

Where to get sounds:
1. Origen Police script (if you have it) - check their html/sounds folder
2. Free police sirens from:
   - https://freesound.org (search "police siren")
   - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/siren/
3. Convert to OGG format using: https://convertio.co/mp3-ogg/

Recommended sound lengths:
- Sirens: 3-10 seconds (they loop automatically)
- Horn: 1-2 seconds

After adding sounds, restart the resource with:
  ensure sb_police
